## Project Overview
This project focuses on the development of a **cloud-native application** designed to provide insights into **drug safety, adverse events, prescription trends, and drug interactions**. The application will integrate multiple datasets to analyze and predict potential risks associated with medications, offering valuable data-driven solutions to healthcare professionals, researchers, and patients.

The application will expose its functionalities through a **REST API**, allowing seamless access to services that analyze and process pharmaceutical data. The project follows a **microservices-based architecture**, ensuring scalability, resilience, and efficiency in cloud environments.

---
## Dataset Information
This project leverages multiple **publicly available datasets** to extract insights related to drug safety and prescription trends. Below are the primary dataset categories used:

### **Adverse Event Reporting & Drug Safety Monitoring**
- **FAERS (FDA Adverse Event Reporting System)** – Reports on drug-related adverse events.
- **EudraVigilance (EMA)** – European pharmacovigilance database for adverse drug reactions.
- **OpenFDA - Drugs** – System for monitoring and analyzing drug safety.
- **VAERS (Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System)** – Data on vaccine adverse events.
- **COVID-19 Vaccine Adverse Reactions** – Reports on adverse effects related to COVID-19 vaccines.

### **Drug Labeling & Regulatory Information**
- **OpenFDA Drug Labeling** – Official drug warnings and label information.
- **United States FDA Drugs Dataset** – Detailed data on FDA-approved drugs.

### **Prescription Trends & Pharmaceutical Sales**
- **CMS Medicare Part D (Prescription Data)** – Trends in prescription drug usage in the U.S.
- **Pharma Sales Data** – Market trends in pharmaceutical product sales.

### **Drug Bioactivity & Interaction Analysis**
- **ChEMBL (Drug Bioactivity & Properties)** – Data on drugs' molecular structures and interactions.
- **Comprehensive Drug Information Dataset** – Information on drug usage, effects, and adverse reactions.

### **Patient-Reported Data & Community Feedback**
- **PatientsLikeMe (Patient Forum)** – User-generated reports on drug side effects, analyzed with NLP.

### **Alternative Drugs & Side Effects**
- **250k Medicines Usage, Side Effects & Substitutes** – Dataset for alternative medications and their side effects.

## Business Capabilities to Be Implemented
The project will implement **key business capabilities** aimed at improving drug safety and prescription transparency:

1. **Drug Safety Monitoring** – Track and analyze reports of adverse drug reactions.
2. **Predictive Analytics for Adverse Events** – Forecast potential side effects based on historical data.
3. **Drug Interaction Risk Identification** – Identify and assess drug interactions for safer prescriptions.

---
## Use Cases
The application will provide several **critical use cases**:

1. **Adverse Reaction Prediction System**
   - Users can input a drug or drug combination to assess risk.
   - Suggest alternative medications with lower risk profiles.
2. **Prescription Trend Analysis Tool**
   - Display the most commonly prescribed drugs and identify high-risk prescription patterns.
3. **Research & Development Support**
   - Enable researchers to identify safer drug candidates.
4. **Patient Education & Information**
   - Provide accessible medication safety information.
   - Allow users to report adverse reactions.
5. **Personalized Safety Alerts for Healthcare Professionals**
   - Enable doctors to assess personalized drug interaction risks.
6. **Automatic Notification System for Pharmacies**
   - Alert pharmacies about drugs with increasing adverse events.
7. **Community Feedback System**
   - Allow users to share medication experiences and detect emerging risks.
8. **Drug Safety Dashboard**
   - Display real-time data on drugs with the most reported adverse effects.
9. **Public Health Monitoring**
   - Help regulatory agencies track trends in drug safety.
